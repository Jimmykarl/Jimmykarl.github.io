Hi! I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Institute for Industrial Research (IFN). My research interest broadly covers environmental and labor economics. I am generally interested in the distributional impacts of the green transition, and how firms respond to environmental regulation.

Currently, my research agenda focuses on the effects of climate policy and green technological change on labor demand and labor market inequality, using administrative data and causal inference methods. In my job market paper, I studied the effects of a carbon tax on firm performance and labor demand in Sweden.
